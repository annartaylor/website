Recent Conference Presentations

In addition to creating visualizations, I present my research regularly at professional meetings. Here are a few recent conference appearances related to my multi-planet atmospheric escape work.

ExoClimes – Poster on my multi-species atmospheric escape model with excited hydrogen and helium.

DPS/EPSC – Oral presentation on coupling lower/middle atmosphere models to a 1D hydrodynamic escape code.

AER Workshop – Talk introducing a proposed scaling relation for He I equivalent width across four exoplanets.

I had the incredible opportunity to present my research at the Exoplanets 5 conference in Leiden, Netherlands! My presentation focused on "Assessment of He I triplet absorption at 10830 Å in escaping atmospheres of hot gaseous exoplanets," with a detailed case study on HD209458b. Exploring the mysteries of exoplanetary atmospheres and has been a thrilling journey, and it was an honor to share my findings with such a distinguished group of scientists and researchers.
